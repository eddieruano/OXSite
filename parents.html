Parents

As a Theta Chi, your son is getting more than a fraternity membership. He's also getting access to top-notch personal development and leadership programs, scholarship support, and access to a global network of academic and professional experience. Theta Chi is more than a fraternity- it's the opportunity of a lifetime.

Our members are so successful in part because of the outstanding leadership and education programming provided by Theta Chi. As a member, you son will have many opportunities to participate in leadership training and personal development programs throughout his college career.

We put this page together to answer as many questions as possible that you may have regarding your son's involvement in a fraternity. That being said, if you do not find all the answers you were looking for please feel free to fill our the additional questions form at the bottom of the page.

  • Dues

Dues are the quarterly fees all members pay. These dues help cover a variety of costs:

  • Insurance Payments
  • Socials such as Semi-Formal, Formal, and exchanges with sororities
  • BrotherHood Events
  • And a spectrum of other activities

  • BrotherHood

We pride ourselves in our strong brotherhood. Theta Chi is like an extended family at college and it is a valuable resource for all of its members. We do a variety of events including but not limited to:

  • Ski Trip to Tahoe or China Peak
  • Camping trip at Lake Nacimiento
  • Paintball
  • Golf
  • Rock Climbing and so many more things!

  • Philanthrophy

We are very active in giving back to the community of San Luis Obispo. We hold a minimum of three community service events and we host a large event with all proceeds benefitting a non-profit organization each quarter.

Parents Weekend

We host an annual parents weekend during Spring Quarter where we invite all of the parents of the brothers to come participate in events, and we inform them about the accomplishments and progress of the year. We also host a silent auction as a fundraiser for the weekend.
