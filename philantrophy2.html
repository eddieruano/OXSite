Philantrophy

Big Brothers Big Sisters


Big Brothers Big Sisters is an organization that provides role models to children facing adversity. The relationships that are forged though this process last lifetimes and allows the children to achieve higher aspirations and build greater confidence.

Theta Chi has been working with BBBS since 2001 and we raise approximately $2000 every year through our fundraising. Furthermore, we volunteer every thursday by sending members to spend time and mentor local children.

Sorority's Best Dance Crew


Sorority's Best Dance Crew, or SBDC, is our most anticipated annual philanthropy event. Each sorority that chooses to participate is paired up with two coaches from Theta Chi. All proceeds go to Big Brothers Big Sisters.



Theta Chi would also like to extend a special thank you to all the sororities and clubs that participated in SBDC 2014.
