A Word From Our President

Theta Chi was originally a chartered fraternity at Cal Poly, founded in 1969. However, they were stripped of their charter by the Grand Chapter of Theta Chi in 1993.

In the winter of 1998 an Interest Group of Theta Chi was created by a core of about 15 students. They did not like the stereotypical “Frat Boy” image that inevitably came with joining a fraternity. Yet they had understood the positive aspects of what a fraternity could offer and wanted to capture that for themselves. They came together with one goal in mind: to start a fraternity superior and different than all the rest. They wanted a fraternity free of hazing, conformity, and inferiority among members. Theta Chi was the fraternity they chose.

On October 25, 1998, we, the new founding fathers of Theta Chi here at Cal Poly, became a colony of 25 members. In order to receive a charter, we had to grow to 45 members strong and show the Grand Chapter we had earned the right to enter into a fraternity that is so well respected throughout the nation and now our school. We reached that goal in April of 2000 and became a nationally recognized chapter of Theta Chi.

We continue to find new challenges and dreams that will continue to develop our brotherhood. We are excited to have your interest and we hope that you want to continue the ideals that we stand for.
